My Story as Yoga teacher

I’m Hellen Ramirez Romero, a passionate yoga teacher specializing in Hatha Yoga and Vinyasa Yoga. My journey with yoga began as a search for balance, and over time, it transformed into a way of life. I believe yoga is more than just movement—it’s a path to self-discovery, healing, and inner peace. Through my teachings, I aim to help others find harmony between the body, mind, and spirit. Whether you’re looking for strength, flexibility, relaxation, or mindfulness, I am here to guide you through a practice that nurtures both physical and emotional well-being.

  • Cultivate Mind-Body Harmony – Through breath, movement, and mindfulness, I help you reconnect with yourself.
  • Using yoga, meditation, and holistic practices to restore balance and vitality.
  • A safe and welcoming space where everyone can grow, heal, and connect.
